Rock Paper Scissors

Rock, paper and scissors is considered one of the oldest, if not the oldest hand game in the world. The earlier versions of this game consisted of a slug, a snake and a frog. Rock, paper and scissors were used in later versions of the game. Some people claim that there's psychological aspects to the game where you could anticipate which one of the tools your opponent is going to use.
